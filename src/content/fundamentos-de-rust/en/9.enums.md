# Enums

Enums (enumerations) in Rust are much more powerful than in other languages. They don't just represent a list of variants — **each variant can contain data of different types and structures**.

Combined with `match`, enums are the central tool for *data modeling* in Rust.

This chapter covers:

- Simple and data-carrying enums
- Exhaustive `match` over enums
- `Option<T>` — the safe replacement for `null`
- `Result<T, E>` — basic error handling
